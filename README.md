# 🚀 Our Hackathon Project

### 📦 AI Powered Inventory Management Platform

Our project focuses on building a **modern intelligent inventory management system** designed to simplify product tracking, warehouse management, and stock monitoring.

The platform aims to help businesses:

- Manage products efficiently
- Monitor inventory levels in real-time
- Track warehouse operations
- Analyze stock performance

---

# 💡 Problem Statement

Many businesses struggle with **inefficient inventory management systems** that cause:

- Poor stock visibility
- Overstock or stock shortages
- Manual tracking errors
- Lack of real-time data

Traditional inventory systems are often **complex, outdated, and difficult to scale**.

---

# 🧠 Our Solution

Team Winarc designed an **AI-powered inventory management system** that provides:

### 📊 Smart Inventory Analytics
Track inventory performance and product trends.

### 📦 Product Lifecycle Management
Manage products from creation to stock updates.

### 🏬 Warehouse Tracking
Monitor multiple warehouse locations with ease.

### 🤖 AI Insights
Analyze data and provide intelligent stock recommendations.

### ⚡ Real-Time Updates
Instant inventory updates across the platform.
